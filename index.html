Services

We offer many types of dog washes, such as the Basic Wash, Wash & Grooming, and Deluxe Spa. In all of our dog washes we will give your dog a nice massage. All of our shampoos are organic and sustainably sourced, so you can ensure that your dog is in good hands.

Appointments

We have several openings for a dog washing appointment. We are open Monday through Saturday, from 9:00am to 4:00pm. The length of our appointments vary by dog washing type, but they are typically an hour long.

Schedule an Appointment

Warranty

We are committed to making sure that you and your dog are satisfied with our services. We want to make sure that your dog has the best experience possible. If you are not satisfied, we will wash your dog again, free of charge until you are.

See our Warranty

About Us

We are a group of students who love dogs and want to share our appreciation for them. We started the business in the beginning of 2022 in hopes to help dog owners and make sure their dogs are being washed with high quality soaps that are sustainably sourced. We are dedicated to giving your dogs a wonderful dog washing experience and making sure that they end up clean and happy.
